Just a reminder to check if a checkbox is checked or not. 

~~~
Feature: Behat bug

  Scenario:
    Given I am on "/bug.html"
    When I check "foo"
    Then the "foo" checkbox should be checked
~~~

or 

~~~
Feature: Behat bug

  Scenario:
    Given I am on "/bug.html"
    When I check "foo"
    Then the "foo" checkbox should not be checked
~~~
